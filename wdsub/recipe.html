BROWNIES

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
    • Rest butter to room temperature
    • wash your hands

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 tsp if baking soda.
    3. Set aside.

  3. Mix wet ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, break two large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract and a half teaspoon of salt to the egg mixture.
    3. Pour a half cup of milk into the wet mix.

  4. Combine mixtures

    1. place the large mixing bowl on a table and mix with mixer on low setting.
    2. Pour wet ingredients in bowl. Keep Mixing until same consistency.
    3. drop room temperature butter in mixer and mix until consistency is fluid

  5. pour and bake

    1. grease a new pan of choice.
    2. Pour mix into pan and smoothe it.
    3. Place in oven and bake for an unreasonable amount of time or until the toothpick has decayed

  6. Enjoy
